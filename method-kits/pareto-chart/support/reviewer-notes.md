# Reviewer Notes: Pareto Method Kit

## Application guidance review

Review status: compact kit reviewed for method-kit usability.

The guide teaches application, not only definition.
It explains the method purpose, QCC stage fit, when to use, when not to use, required inputs, output, procedure through the manual recipe, interpretation, common mistakes, review checklist, evidence note, and related methods.

The chart-creation sections give executable manual chart steps for categories and counts, one consistent period and scope, descending count sort, column bars, optional cumulative percentage line, formatting, annotations, interpretation, defects, and evidence note.

The interpretation sections give safe conclusion wording and prevent root-cause overclaims.
The review checklist provides pass/fail checks for chart construction and evidence readiness.

## Teaching visual review

No binary teaching visuals are included.
The good and bad examples are reviewed Markdown teaching notes.
They are conceptual only and not final project evidence.

## Structure review

The kit uses `guide.md` as the single primary user-facing document.
Prompts, evidence-note template, teaching-example review notes, and reviewer notes live in `support/` so users are not forced through many files before applying the method.
