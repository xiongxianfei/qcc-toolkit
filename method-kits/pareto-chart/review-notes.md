# Review Notes: Pareto Method Kit

## Application guidance review

Review status: draft kit reviewed for M2 implementation handoff.

The method-guide teaches application, not only definition.
It explains the method purpose, QCC stage fit, when to use, when not to use, required inputs, output, procedure through the manual recipe, interpretation, common mistakes, review checklist, evidence note, and related methods.

The chart-creation guide gives executable manual chart steps for categories and counts, one consistent period and scope, descending count sort, column bars, optional cumulative percentage line, formatting, annotations, interpretation, defects, and evidence note.

The interpretation guide gives safe conclusion wording and prevents root-cause overclaims.
The review checklist provides pass/fail checks for chart construction and evidence readiness.
